# Three O'Clock — Brand Brief
*One-page brief · Workshop example (Block 1 output)*

**One-sentence concept:** A subscription snack box engineered for the 3pm desk slump — single-serve, protein-and-fiber portions that give you steady afternoon focus without the sugar crash.

**Target customer:** Office and remote knowledge workers, roughly 28–45, who power through back-to-back afternoons, keep reaching for the vending machine or candy drawer around 3pm, and then regret the crash and the cleanup. They care about focus and feeling good more than strict "clean eating."

**Problem we solve:** The afternoon energy dip quietly tanks productivity. The easy fixes — candy, chips, a fourth coffee — spike you, then drop you harder. There's no convenient option built for *sustained* focus and portioned so you don't mindlessly eat the whole bag at your desk.

## Product line
- **The 3pm Box** — monthly subscription of 20 single-serve snacks, each balanced for protein + fiber and capped at low added sugar.
- **Slump-tested picks** — savory and lightly-sweet options (roasted chickpeas, nut-and-seed clusters, jerky, dark-chocolate-and-almond bites) that satisfy a real craving, not a "diet" version of it.
- **Desk-friendly format** — flat, single-hand, no-mess packets that fit a drawer and don't crumble on a keyboard.
- **Build-your-box** — swap or rate snacks each month so the mix tunes to your taste over time.
- **Office plan** — multi-seat subscription for teams that want a better break-room option than the candy bowl.

## Brand voice
- **Knowing, not preachy** — talks like a sharp coworker, never a wellness lecture.
- **Practical** — concrete about energy, focus, and timing instead of vague "clean" buzzwords.
- **Dry wit** — light humor about the 3pm wall and the vending-machine walk of shame.
- **Reassuring** — confident that small, well-timed choices beat willpower.

## 3 launch-message ideas
1. **"Beat the 3pm wall."** The snack built for your afternoon, not your sweet tooth.
2. **"One snack. Zero crash."** Portioned for focus, so you finish the day strong — not slumped.
3. **"Your candy drawer called. It's been replaced."** 20 single-serve picks that actually keep you going.
