# Sprig — Brand Brief
*One-page brief · Workshop example (Block 1 output)*

**One-sentence concept:** Sprig is a foolproof starter kit for first-time plant owners — a hardy plant, the correctly sized pot, and a care card that texts you exactly when to water, so nothing dies in week three.

**Target customer:** Renters and new homeowners aged 25–40 who want their space to feel more alive but have killed a plant before (or assume they will). They're design-conscious, busy, and want a low-stakes win — not a hobby.

**Problem we solve:** Beginners overwater, underwater, and guess at light. Generic plants and pots arrive with no instructions, the plant dies, and they conclude they have a "black thumb." We remove the guesswork and the shame.

## Product line
- **The Starter Kit** — one beginner-proof plant (pothos, snake plant, ZZ), a drainage pot matched to its size, and a care card.
- **Care card + text reminders** — scan the card to get watering and light nudges by SMS, timed to your specific plant and home.
- **Tiered difficulty** — "Unkillable," "Confident," and "Ready for More" kits so customers level up instead of starting over.
- **Refill & swap** — replacement plants at a member price if one doesn't make it, no questions asked.
- **Gift kits** — pre-set bundles for housewarmings and new-apartment gifts, shipped with a personal note.

## Brand voice
- **Reassuring** — we assume you're new, never that you're careless.
- **Plainspoken** — real instructions in plain English, zero Latin plant names or jargon.
- **Lightly witty** — warm and a little funny, never precious or preachy about plants.
- **Honest** — we tell you what's actually hard and what isn't, so you trust the easy parts.

## 3 launch-message ideas
1. **"You're not bad with plants. You just never had the manual."** — Sprig sends the manual to your phone.
2. **"The plant that texts you before it's thirsty."** — Start with one you literally can't forget to water.
3. **"Your first plant, engineered not to die."** — Right plant, right pot, reminders included. Win on the easy setting.
